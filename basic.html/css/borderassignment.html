Q:-Who is veer Teja ji ?
ans:-Veer Teja Ji, also known as Tejaji Maharaj, is a Rajasthani folk deity and one of the 11 major incarnations of Shiva. He was born on January 29, 1074 in Kharnal, Rajasthan to parents Ramkunwari and Tahar, who were Jats. Tejaji is worshipped throughout Rajasthan, both in rural and urban areas.


Q:- Who is Maharana Pratap
Ans:- Pratap Singh I, popularly known as Maharana Pratap, was a king of Kingdom of Mewar, in north-western India in the present-day state of Rajasthan. He is notable for leading the Rajput resistance against the expansionist policy of the Mughal Emperor Akbar including the Battle of Haldighati.


What is HTML
HTML, or Hypertext Markup Language, is the standard markup language used to create and structure web pages. It uses tags to define the content and structure of a web page, which tells the browser how to display the content. For example, HTML elements can label pieces of content as headings, paragraphs, or links.
